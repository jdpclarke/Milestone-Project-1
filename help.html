Submit a Request to Assess Your Eligibility

Ready to unlock your learning potential? Find Out if You Qualify for ASF Funding! Submit our quick and easy eligibility assessment form today to see if you could be eligible for Adult Skills Fund (ASF) funding. We'll ask you a few simple questions about your age, qualifications, employment status, and the type of course you're interested in. Based on your answers, we'll give you an initial indication of your eligibility.

Important Note: While we strive to provide helpful information, this website and its assessment tool are not a substitute for professional advice. The specific funding you can access will depend on your individual circumstances, and factors such as your age, prior qualifications, employment status, and chosen course all play a role. Therefore, this assessment is for informational purposes only and does not constitute a guarantee of funding. For personalised advice tailored to your situation, we strongly recommend speaking with a certified training provider or careers advisor. We are not certified experts on ASF funding and cannot accept any liability for the information provided on this website.

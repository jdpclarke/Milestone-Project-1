Start a new chapter!

The guidance on this page will support you in taking that first step towards your education journey. The many benefits of the Adult Skills Fund (ASF) will support you in financing your chosen path.While the free courses and focus on employment are great, there are some other features of the ASF that benefit learners:

  • Flexibility
  • Accessibility
  • Progression
  • Focus on Skills Needs

Note: It's important to remember that with ASF, and the specific benefits available will depend on individual circumstances and the courses you're interested in. It's always a good idea to talk to a training provider or careers advisor to get personalised advice.
